How To Get To Us

Lost? Need Directions? If you are Travelling from Hamilton, Find Route 1 and head towards Te Rapa. If you are heading that way you should be heading towards Auckland. When you get to the Highway bridge just before Taupiri make a turn onto Route 18. Keep going then once you make it to the Tainui Bridge, take a left and drive over it. Then follow the road until you get to Waikokowai.

If you are travelling from Auckland, Drive south on Route 1 Highway towards Hamilton. Keep Following the Route 1 highway until you get to Rangiriri. Then on the roundabout take the 3rd exit onto Flen Murray Rd and follow that road until Rotongaro Rd. If you remain on this road driving south then you will find Waikokowai Rd and when you follow that you will end up at Waikokowai
